Heroes

To obtain a positive trait, a Player has to win a series of consecutive battles in the primary campaign or send a particular Hero to the Tavern to get a trait at the Card table. Players can obtain a random trait, either positive or negative, though the chances to get the negative ones are lower. Heroes can get rid of a negative trait In the Bedchamber. When Heroes are in the Tavern, they are not available for combat. It is always possible for them to get back into battle when Players remove Heroes from the Tavern without acquiring a trait; or speed up the process in exchange for gems.

Use gems to reset a Hero in exchange for resources. Proceed to the Temple to reset a Hero level. Find a 'Reset' option, select a Hero, and tap 'Reset.' After resetting a Hero, their gear and accumulated resources and experience will be returned. The level of Hero Ascension is not subject to change after resetting.

To ascend a Hero, players should sacrifice a certain number of other Heroes. Ascension requirements can be different and depend on the current level of Ascension. Heroes chosen for the sacrifice will be scattered and lost forever. All their gear, experience, and resources will be returned to the player. Additionally, to ascend Heroes, you can use Universal Souls to substitute for the third Hero with a particular level of ascension. Note that Fallen Heroes cannot be ascended.

To raise the Ascension level of a Hero, you need to collect three identical Heroes. Enter the Temple and find a 'Merge' option. Select the identical Heroes and tap the "Merge' button to reach the next level of Ascension.

Here's an essential key to success: you need to level up your Heroes, raise their Ascension level and develop an effective strategy for using spells. Moreover, it's crucial to upgrade the spells and equipment regularly. It can also be helpful to reconsider the set of Heroes you tend to use in accordance with your combat style and objectives.

If a Hero is locked and cannot be selected as a team member, it is recommended to check other game activities, for example, the Tavern. Please note that when Heroes are currently engaged in the Tavern, Bedchamber, or Card table, they are not available for combat.

When summoning a new Hero at the Recruitment Point, players can obtain Fallen Heroes instead of regular ones. You can purify them at the sacred Altar in the Temple. After the purification, players receive Seals of the Fallen. Fallen Heroes are not featured in the Hero Collection and cannot be ascended. If not purified, they can be used as team members and upgraded as they have their special battle abilities and can benefit the cause.

Seals of the Fallen appear when summoning a Fallen Hero and can be used at the Mercenary Shop. Once you purify a Fallen Hero at the Altar, they disappear while you immediately receive the Seals. At the moment, players can buy Hero Scrolls, Soul Vessels, and Hero Shards of all classes at the Mercenary Shop in exchange for Fallen Seals.

The collection features all Heroes divided into classes, except Fallen Heroes, who are not available there. When you get a Hero that is not in your collection yet, you receive rewards and exp. Exp and rewards are also given after ascending a Hero to a new grade. Exp is required to upgrade class levels that improve the bonus characteristics of Heroes. Bonuses are applied to all Heroes of the corresponding class. Obtain new Heroes and ascend them to increase the overall power of your squad.

Players can use Summoning Scrolls at the Recruitment Point. There is a 100% chance that you will summon a Hero. Fallen Heroes and Soul Vessels do not appear when players use Summoning Scrolls.

Players can obtain new Heroes while completing the challenges of the main campaign, collecting the 28-day bonus (7th and 28th day), and summoning them at the Recruitment Point. It is possible to summon them in exchange for gems and Hero Scrolls. If you use gems, you can receive Heroes, Fallen Heroes, or Universal Souls of various grades. Summoning Scrolls offer a 100% chance of collecting a Hero. The quickest way to get the Hero that you want is by looking closely at the special offers that appear from time to time.

Tap on the icon of the Hero you intend to remove from the squad while selecting the members before the battle. Once you do this, you will see all Heroes available for battle. Tap the one you need, and this Hero will occupy vacant space. Note that it is not possible to use two identical Heroes in a team at the same time. Moreover, some Heroes cannot be selected if they are currently in the Tavern or engaged in other game activities.

Players can obtain Summoning Scrolls while completing challenges of the main campaign; in the Split of the worlds; and find them in the Royal Pass rewards. The quickest way to receive the Scrolls is by purchasing them in the game bank or unlocking the Royal Pass premium access.

Resources

Mana is accumulated in the mimic chest over time and given to a player with other rewards. Those who are not inclined to be waiting can easily refill mana points in exchange for gems. To do this, tap on the '+' button next to the mana bar indicating your mana points, then tap 'Replenish.' You can find your mana pool indicator in a window that allows selecting Heroes before the battle. It is also possible to refill mana points in the Tower of Magic. You need to tap on the '+' button next to the mana bar and then tap 'Replenish.'

Players can obtain spells as rewards for completing quests; in the Split of the world, they can also purchase spells in the Magic Shop, Stone Town. Magic Shop is only available after you have completed Stage 4.1.

There is no capacity limit for the bag at the moment. You can store as many items as you like.

Hero Medals are used to upgrade heroes. They are required when you need to level up a hero - from 10 to 11 level, 20 to 21, etc.

A Soul Vessel can give you a Universal Soul of any Ascension grade.

Use a Universal Soul while merging heroes to substitute for any 3rd hero of a certain Ascension grade. Players can obtain Universal Souls at the Recruitment Point or from the Soul Vessel, which can be acquired in the Split of the Worlds. Souls and Soul Vessels can also be purchased in special offers.

Various items such as Hiking Tools, Holy Water, Medicinal Herbs, a Pick Set, Torches, etc., are occasionally required while exploring the terrain, taking part in the battle events, and completing the tasks in the Labyrinth of Madness. Each battle event demands a particular item useful in this or that situation.

Players receive gems while completing certain daily quests and particular challenges in the main campaign; find them in the Utopia of Dragons rewards; in the daily bonus (Bank, daily offers); in the in-game mail gifts; the Tower of Darkness rewards. The quickest way to get more gems is by making a purchase in the bank, purchasing a Monthly Card, and getting the Royal Pass premium access.

A certain number of runes allows you to upgrade the spells of a particular Magic School. There are four different types of Magic Runes blessed with different types of magic - light, dark, chaos, and summoning magic. Players can obtain Runes in the Utopia of Dragons rewards after defeating a dragon of a certain type.

World Map Adventures

You can use Heroes in any activities offered in the adventures, but note that the same Hero can not be engaged in more than one activity at a time. You can send this Hero to a new battle only after the previous activity has been completed.

You need Heroes of a particular level and grade to take part in the Mines of Abyss activities. The challenges are updated every day and gradually get more advanced as they are completed. With each level of activity, the requirements for the Heroes increase, as well as the reward.

In the Towers of Darkness, you can encounter cursed heroes and monstrous bosses. The tower is not affected by magic; that is why you cannot use spells here.

Players only receive rewards if Heroes were defeated in the battle or if the activity timer has expired. If you deliberately quit the battle, you will not receive the rewards.

Upon defeating a dragon, players are entitled to receive a chest containing the rewards, after which the dragon becomes more powerful and increases its level. There are three types of chests with different sets of rewards and different probabilities of obtaining them. The higher the level of the dragon, the better the reward.

There's a special system of torches in the Labyrinth of Madness. The level of light affects the enemies and their combat skills, your rewards, and the scouting chance. The lower the level of light, the higher the ATK and DEF of the enemies, but the better the reward. The scouting chance affects the opportunities to reveal which activity in a room will be the first to participate. It decreases together with the level of light. A torch goes out as long as Heroes take part in a battle and labyrinth battle events; while entering a new room or a new tier. It's possible to increase the level of light by tapping on the torch. Players can purchase torches at the market and obtain them in the Labyrinth battle events.

The Labyrinth of Madness gets reset every 48 hours, offering rooms with various enemies, bosses, and new activities. Make sure you've got enough supplies, and select your Heroes wisely. It's not possible to make changes in your squad until the Labyrinth has been reset. Choose your route carefully while selecting a room to enter, as you cannot go back and head to another room. Each Labyrinth has three tiers. Having completed the tier, Heroes have an opportunity to set up a camp to have some rest. A camp serves as a checkpoint - next time you enter the Labyrinth, you will have to start at the last camp you have reached. Blessings obtained in the previous tiers are retained. Players can restore some HP, resurrect Heroes fallen in a battle against evil, and acquire and upgrade blessings. The level of difficulty of each tier rises gradually. In the heart of the Labyrinth, enemies become more powerful, while traps and obstacles are much more dangerous. Be aware that Heroes in your squad might frequently die, after which you will have to start the tier all over again.

Royal Pass

The Royal Pass is a limited-time reward system. After completing daily quests, players receive Royal Points. They are accumulated and displayed in the vertical progress bar. Once the progress indicator reaches a particular level, you will be able to collect the rewards on the left. After the Royal Pass cycle has finished, all progress will be updated. The rewards you have not collected will reset.

Once players unlock access to premium rewards, after reaching the required levels of Royal Pass progress, they are entitled to receive a basic set of rewards together with elite rewards. Premium access is only active for one billing cycle.

Yes, you can unlock premium access any day during an active Royal Pass cycle and are entitled to receive the rewards that will be available according to your progress and the number of Royal Points collected.

Additionally, more gems (according to the type of Card) will be delivered via in-game mail every day, starting from the first day within 30 days. After making the purchase, you can receive a one-time purchase reward. Having acquired a Lux Monthly Card, which proves to be highly beneficial, players receive a bigger one-time reward and more gems daily.
